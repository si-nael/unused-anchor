# v0.5.3 Persistent Causal Structure

Status: completed bounded persistence release on 2026-07-15

## Why This Release Exists

`v0.5.2` proves that an actor-neutral Bubble can generate exact causal events and an emergent structure from its own laws. Its laws still apply only once per realization, so one finite closure cannot establish that the structure remains itself, remembers, repairs damage, or affects its surroundings across later lawful closure. Calling that structure a being or agent would therefore be premature.

`v0.5.3` adds a separate persistence layer over the unchanged `bubble-anchored-causal-program.v2`. It discovers recurrent causal components from formal dependencies, unfolds every lawful selected continuation, and promotes no component unless boundary, identity, memory, self-maintenance, and outward causal influence all have executable evidence.

No entity, protagonist, agent, or persistence criterion is declared by the canonical program. The runtime derives candidates and then tests a bounded evidence contract.

## Closure Coalgebra Without Universal Time

Let `C` be the exact product of all causal world fields and `H*` the append-only anchor-history ledger. One completed `v0.5.2` realization has the bounded shape

`R : C -> P_f(C x DeltaH*) + U + K`,

where `P_f` contains every lawfully selected continuation and its history extension, `U` is an unresolved resource or semantic frontier, and `K` is contradiction. The persistence runtime coinductively unfolds `R`; it never chooses one member of `P_f`.

Each unfolding is recorded as a causal closure, not a time tick. A configuration-continuation edge proves that the output configuration of one closure is the exact input configuration of the next. Every irreversible commit is separately appended to `H*`; `universalClock` remains false. At least two closures are required before a recurrence claim can be certified.

A finite configuration lasso is certified when exact configuration digests satisfy `c_i = c_j` for `i < j`. This is recurrence in the projection `pi_C(C x H*)`, not an assertion that accumulated anchor history repeats. The cycle certificate records history-ledger cardinality at both cuts, its monotone extension, and whether the full anchored state also repeated. The canonical world extends history, so its full anchored state does not repeat. Resource exhaustion before recurrence is `undetermined`, not evidence of persistence or dissolution.

## Boundary From Causal Dependence

Internal laws induce a directed conjunctive dependency hypergraph. The executable factor graph retains separate field and law nodes, `field -> law -> field`, so a law's complete guard tuple remains inspectable instead of being mistaken for independent single-field causal sufficiency. Strongly connected field projections are derived from that factor graph rather than declared as entities.

For a component `S`, the runtime records

- the incoming law hypercut `delta-(S)`, whose guard tuple has an outside member and whose effects enter `S`;
- the outgoing law hypercut `delta+(S)`, whose guard tuple has an inside member and whose effects leave `S`;
- the field roles contained in `S`.

The bounded persistence detector considers only single-world recurrent components containing structural, identity, memory, and boundary-bearing fields. This is a detection contract, not a claim that those four labels are a final theory of life. A certified boundary requires non-empty cuts and every crossing law to bind a boundary-bearing member in its guard. The runtime then restores the boundary field to its pre-formation value, suppresses unrelated laws that would merely recreate it, and requires every crossing law's realization to change on every lawful counterfactual continuation. A syntactically present but semantically redundant boundary guard therefore fails. Mixed ablation evidence remains `undetermined`.

## Exact Stronger-Emergence Evidence

A component is `persistent` only when all five surfaces hold on every lawful recurrent path:

1. **Boundary:** a two-sided conjunctive law hypercut is fully boundary-mediated and pre-formation boundary-state ablation changes every crossing law on every lawful anchored continuation.
2. **Identity:** exact configuration recurrence exists and identity-bearing fields remain invariant at every cut in the certified loop.
3. **Memory:** replacing retained memory with its pre-formation value changes every next lawful continuation. Mixed counterfactual branches are unresolved, never collapsed into an existential positive.
4. **Self-maintenance:** an exact negative-sea increase causes a structural deviation away from the recurrent reference configuration, followed by an internal restoration to that exact reference through a law whose guard depends on boundary and retained memory. `Erosion` is used only when the authored structural interpretation supplies that direction.
5. **Causal influence:** a realized law crosses the outgoing cut and every memory-erasure continuation changes at least one external field.

The conjunction is deliberately stronger than a generic emergence predicate:

`persistent(S) = boundary(S) and identity(S) and memory(S) and maintenance(S) and influence(S)`.

It still does not establish consciousness, agency, a protagonist, a goal, a relationship, or a story.

## Canonical World

[`examples/self-maintaining-field.world.json`](../../examples/self-maintaining-field.world.json) contains no protagonist or entity declaration. Its laws produce this exact chain:

`formation -> negative-sea admission -> sea-caused erosion -> memory-and-boundary restoration`.

The negative sea is then released by a separate commuting law. The formed causal configuration recurs after two closures with a one-closure loop. Each closure also appends a distinct restoration commit to anchor history, so the full anchored state is correctly reported as non-repeating. The derived recurrent component contains boundary, identity, integrity, memory, and repair phase; negative sea and an outward signal remain outside its boundary-mediated hypercut.

Erasing retained memory prevents restoration, changes the external signal, and leaves the structure damaged. Increasing erosion beyond the authored restoration law also prevents certification. Disabling World Will does not remove the autonomous maintenance cycle.

## No Hidden Scheduler And No Immortality

- every selected plural continuation is unfolded; no host winner is chosen;
- plural memory and cut-ablation counterfactuals are universally quantified; mixed evidence remains unresolved;
- an exact plural-tie regression produces two persistence paths and requires both to pass;
- non-commuting or unresolved underlying closures remain unresolved;
- closure and path bounds are explicit proof resources;
- a contradiction remains contradiction at the persistence layer;
- the structure may dissolve under pressure outside its restoration capacity;
- the host records proof unfolding but does not install a world clock.

## Compatibility And Non-Closure

`v0.5.0`, `v0.5.1`, and `v0.5.2` semantics remain unchanged. The persistence program wraps the v0.5.2 causal program rather than mutating its once-per-realization meaning.

This release closes one bounded persistence-and-boundary case. It does not derive goals, counterfactual choice among affordances, relationships, narrative projection, dynamic field schemas, spatial or evolutionary populations, global multi-world sea transport, cross-world anchors, the unresolved `고유 세계` topology, or recursive self-construction. OB-001 remains open.
