# v0.5.1 Anchored Narrative World Architecture

Status: completed bounded connected release on 2026-07-15  
Recorded: 2026-07-15

## Objective

`v0.5.1` establishes the first exact connected transition from the bounded `v0.5.0` state-candidate kernel toward a genuine Bubble universe. Protagonists, story, law, both seas, anchors, and World Will have distinct causal roles but execute as one typed structure.

This is not an in-world menu of mathematical regimes. Advanced mathematics belongs to the host implementation and proof machinery. A Bubble may have its own laws, but the runtime must not ask the Bubble to choose whether its host uses category theory, causal models, control, or another implementation technique.

The discarded direction of attaching several semantic regimes and choosing among them is explicitly outside this architecture. Organic realization requires coupled necessity, not a presentation-layer candidate menu.

## Three Semantic Layers

### 1. Author idea and provenance layer

Natural language is permitted here because it is the best surface for preserving the author's evolving ideas, questions, imagery, and corrections. It is never executable truth by itself.

Every promoted idea keeps:

- immutable source text and date
- a separate engineering interpretation
- implementation obligations and dependencies
- honest status
- code and test evidence
- remaining limits and a reopen condition

The canonical current sources are [../research/author-idea-corpus.md](../research/author-idea-corpus.md) and `data/research/idea-obligations.v1.json`.

### 2. Formal world kernel

Executable meaning is a typed, versioned formal object with exact validation rules. The first `v0.5.1` component is `bubble-intensional-system.v1` in `src/bubbles/world-kernel/intensional.ts`.

A finite definition denotes an indexed family

\[
f : I \to V,
\]

where `I` may contain natural, integer, finite, or finite-product axes and `V` is currently an exact rational, boolean, or symbolic value space. If one axis is natural or integer, the denotation is countably infinite even though the source is finite.

An infinite-dimensional vector is represented as `x : N -> V`. An infinite matrix or operator begins as `A : N x N -> V`. The runtime evaluates only a demanded coordinate. It never claims to have enumerated the full family.

Recursive families require a declared natural-number measure and every self-demand must syntactically reduce that measure by a positive integer. Cross-family cycles without a well-founded measure are rejected. A finite evaluation budget is an epistemic and computational limit: exhaustion yields `undetermined`, never a fabricated value.

The kernel records a coordinate proof containing the exact demand, dependencies, recursion rule, and result status. Exact rational arithmetic avoids floating-point drift in the current supported fragment.

This first fragment does not yet support uncountable domains, symbolic limits, infinite sums or integrals, coinductive streams, higher-order operators, probabilistic measures, or lowering from narrative Bubble syntax. Those are live extension obligations, not implied features.

### 3. Authored Bubble surface

Bubble source remains a constrained formal DSL. Human-readable names and descriptions may coexist with it, but a statement becomes executable only after deterministic lowering into the formal kernel. NLP may later assist authoring or propose formalizations; it must never silently decide law, identity, agency, or history.

New surface syntax is deliberately deferred until the narrative and intervention IR is stable enough that syntax would expose real semantics instead of freezing a metaphor.

## Connected World Model

For one bubble universe `i`, the working external model is

\[
U_i = (P_i, G_i, X_i, L_i, E_i, H_i, B_i, A_i),
\]

where:

- `P_i` is the set or generated family of protagonists and other beings
- `G_i` carries goals, commitments, relationships, and agency constraints
- `X_i` is the intensional world and story state
- `L_i` is local law
- `E_i` is a causal event structure
- `H_i` is committed history, which may remain empty
- `B_i` is the membrane or boundary
- `A_i` is the set or family of anchors touching the world

The positive and negative seas are wider relational fields `S+` and `S-`. They are not scalar decorations attached independently to each world. Their local projections may influence viability, continuity, membrane pressure, placement, and the cost or availability of intervention.

World Will is an objective-and-constraint structure `W`, not an external chooser:

\[
u^* \in \operatorname*{arg\,max}_{u \in \mathcal{U}(A, B)}
J(U_1, \ldots, U_n, S^+, S^-, u) - C(u)
\]

subject to identity, agency, boundary, and history constraints. The `v0.5.1` runtime implements this obligation exactly for a finite intervention frontier by exhaustively evaluating admissible intervention combinations up to an explicit resource bound. Budget exhaustion yields `underdetermined`; it never authorizes a convenient host choice. If several interventions remain equally lawful, the world stays plural or underdetermined according to authored semantics.

World evolution has the form

\[
U_i' = F_i(U_i, \operatorname{act}(P_i, G_i, X_i), A_i(u_i), S^+, S^-).
\]

Protagonist action appears separately from World Will intervention. World Will may alter conditions, opportunities, constraints, signals, resource gradients, or boundary pressure through an anchor. It may not directly overwrite a protagonist's choice and still claim preserved agency.

## Anchor Contract

An anchor is not a checksum or a coarse strength score. The full intended contract has four inseparable roles:

1. identity witness: what must remain invariant for this world to remain itself
2. causal channel: how lawful cross-world or World-Will influence reaches the world
3. provenance carrier: which origin, intervention, sea condition, and prior event caused a change
4. permission boundary: which influences may cross without violating agency, membrane, or committed history

Every World-Will intervention in the future narrative kernel must name an anchor route. Cutting that route must remove the intervention while leaving unrelated internal protagonist action possible. If the same result occurs after the anchor is cut, the test must expose either an internal cause or a falsely modeled intervention.

The author's phrase `고유 세계` is intentionally unresolved. This architecture does not install one absolute privileged original. It permits lineage-relative and relational addresses until the author settles whether the intended structure is a unique core world, one base per relation, a fixed point, or another topology.

## Story and Agency Contract

A story is not a prose field. Its executable `v0.5.1` minimum requires:

- protagonists with persistent identity
- goals or commitments that can conflict or align
- relationships that change causal affordances
- lawful actions attributed to protagonists
- consequences in a causal event graph
- unresolved, reversible, and committed story state
- autonomous continuation when World Will is disabled
- inspectable World-Will intervention when it is enabled

Internal protagonists belong to Phase 1 because they constitute the world. The deferred Phase 2 observer or benchmark agent remains outside the world and must not be confused with them.

Agency preservation needs counterfactual evidence. At minimum, tests must compare:

- World Will enabled versus disabled
- anchor connected versus cut
- objective alignment versus objective conflict
- positive-sea support versus negative-sea pressure
- protagonist action available versus prohibited by local law

The desired result is neither total World-Will control nor a disconnected story. Intervention changes lawful conditions; protagonists still produce attributable actions; the coupled story changes for intelligible causal reasons.

## Order Without Forced Time

The kernel assumes no universal scalar clock. Narrative events form a causal relation `e_a < e_b` when one event is a necessary predecessor of another. Independent events remain unordered in the world relation even though the host records a deterministic evaluation sequence. Reversible events do not create committed history. Only an irreversible commitment creates a durable history arrow. General cyclic trajectories remain beyond this acyclic release kernel.

Host execution order is not automatically world time. A proof or replay record must distinguish evaluation sequence, causal order, and committed-history order.

## v0.5.1 Completion Gates

The working version must not be called complete until all connected gates below have executable evidence:

1. Author quotations and obligations are preserved and automatically validated.
2. Finite formal definitions can denote and query required unbounded or infinite-dimensional world fields without eager expansion.
3. Protagonist, goal, relationship, story-state, and causal-event IR exists without relying on prose interpretation.
4. World Will has explicit objectives, costs, and hard constraints over the connected world state.
5. Every intervention is anchor-mediated and has causal provenance.
6. A story can continue from protagonist action with World Will disabled.
7. World-Will intervention changes conditions without directly replacing protagonist choice.
8. Positive and negative sea state materially changes a viability or intervention result through an inspectable path.
9. Causal partial order, reversibility, and committed history remain distinguishable without a universal clock.
10. Inspection and replay preserve the same identities, causes, and unresolved alternatives.
11. Counterfactual tests for disabled Will, cut anchor, objective conflict, and sea pressure pass.
12. No example-specific branch or hard-coded protagonist is required for the semantics.
13. Full repository verification passes and the implementation map states every remaining limit.

As of 2026-07-15, all thirteen gates have bounded executable evidence:

1. `author-idea-corpus.md`, `idea-obligations.v1.json`, and research-record tests preserve quotations, dates, limits, and reopen conditions.
2. `intensional.ts` denotes exact countably infinite indexed families and evaluates only demanded coordinates with proof and resource evidence.
3. `narrative.ts` types protagonists, goals, relationships, story state, and causal events without prose interpretation; `narrative-runtime.ts` executes their finite field-and-event seam while relationship-dependent affordances remain a stated limit.
4. World-Will candidates are scored from exact connected world state after cost, and state-bound formal hard constraints are checked after each candidate transition.
5. Executable interventions require an identity-validated, uncut, permission-valid anchor and carry anchor and intervention identifiers in causal trace.
6. Non-intervention causal closure realizes protagonist action with World Will disabled.
7. Validation forbids World Will from mutating protagonist-choice and story fields; intervention changes lawful conditions before attributable protagonist consequences execute.
8. Exact positive/negative sea projection changes viability after every relevant transition and remains visible in trace.
9. Causal edges, host evaluation order, reversible event membership, irreversible event membership, and commit-created history arrows are recorded separately with `universalClock: false`.
10. Inspection and deterministic re-execution preserve program identity, selected continuations, unresolved alternatives, causal traces, and history commits.
11. Focused tests cover disabled Will, cut anchor, objective conflict, post-state hard constraints, and negative-sea pressure.
12. The runtime contains no fixture-specific protagonist or branch; the canonical garden is an external formal JSON program.
13. The mandatory repository verification path includes the canonical run/inspect/record/replay cycle, and the implementation map keeps wider limits open.

This certifies the bounded `v0.5.1` connected seam, not the complete Bubble universe promised by OB-001.

## Explicitly Deferred Post-Completion Horizon

Recursive Bubble self-construction is preserved but excluded from this release. A later Bubble may generate modules, synthesize formally admissible code, execute verified code, and construct a successor representation of itself. Existing meta-language and intensional structures are possible bootstrap material only.

This direction reopens after the current Bubble universe and all prior idea obligations are complete. It must not be used to generate around missing protagonist, story, World-Will, anchor, sea, identity, or replay semantics.

## Preserved Reopen Frontier

The completed seam is deliberately finite and exact. It does not yet provide persistent open-ended story execution across records, relationship-dependent affordance laws, a global multi-world sea field, cross-world anchor transfer, a complete anchor identity calculus, cyclic or coinductive causal trajectories, uncountable or measure-valued fields, or authored Bubble-syntax lowering into this kernel.

Those limits remain obligations around the completed release. They must be reopened from a concrete representation or world-coherence failure and must not be erased by the bounded completion label.
