# Trans-Turing Bubble Semantics

Date: 2026-07-17

Status: open independent research question; no hypercomputation capability is claimed by the current runtime

## Foundational Question

Can a Bubble universe explicitly or implicitly exceed the limits of a Turing machine without hiding an oracle inside a number, treating unbounded representation as completed computation, or letting World Will, an anchor, a sea, nondeterminism, or self-reference act as unexplained magic?

This question must separate at least four meanings of "beyond Turing":

| Meaning | Possible in Bubble? | Current status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A finite host program effectively computes a non-recursive function | Only with a non-Turing resource or assumption | Not implemented and not claimed |
| A finite Bubble definition denotes an infinite or non-computable mathematical object | Yes, as intensional or axiomatic denotation | Countable computable families exist; non-computable denotation is not typed |
| A world computes relative to an oracle reached through a causal boundary | Formally possible | No oracle or computability-degree contract exists |
| A world has transfinite, exact-continuum, or physically hypercomputational dynamics | Model-dependent | No limit-stage or physical-substrate semantics exists |

## Current Honest Boundary

The current TypeScript executor, exact rational arithmetic, guarded natural recursion, finite proof budgets, causal closure, teleonomic analysis, and finite maximal-frontier branching remain effectively computable. Self-modification, plural branching, host parallelism, randomness, and recursive world generation would not cross the Turing boundary while every transition and input remains computable.

Bubble already has a narrower but important capability: a finite typed definition can denote a countably infinite or infinite-dimensional family, and a run materializes only demanded coordinates. This raises the representability frontier but does not decide a non-computable predicate. Budget exhaustion remains `undetermined` instead of becoming a fabricated answer.

The full denotation of a world may also have global properties that no internal being or host algorithm can decide. That is an epistemic or semantic limit inside a world, not evidence that the executor performed hypercomputation.

## Candidate Research Models

### Relative oracle anchors

An anchor could eventually carry a typed computational relation between worlds. A world on one side might query a source whose answers are not computable within that world's effective law. The result would be computation relative to an explicitly named oracle or degree, not a claim that the Bubble created non-computable information from nothing.

This is only one possible capability of an anchor and must not redefine every anchor as an oracle. Any such result would require source identity, causal route, query, answer, assumed degree, trust, cost, and replay provenance.

### Transfinite causal order

Bubble's rejection of a universal scalar clock leaves room for successor and limit positions in a causal partial order. A limit transition could be defined by a fixed-point, colimit, `liminf`, or another typed completion rule. An ordinal causal rank is not automatically physical duration.

A finite host can manipulate a symbolic limit proof or a finite certificate, but it cannot honestly claim to have completed infinitely many effective steps. Actual transfinite execution would therefore need an explicit supertask assumption, an oracle, or a non-host physical substrate.

### Exact continuum semantics

A model with exact arbitrary real values may have different computational power from a bit-based machine. It is unsafe to treat this as a free implementation upgrade: a non-computable real can contain an unbounded non-computable answer stream. Bubble would have to record how the real is specified, prepared, measured, and distinguished from a disguised oracle.

### Axiomatic or model-theoretic worldhood

A Bubble may be defined by the model or fixed point satisfying its laws rather than by an executable enumeration of every fact. In that case a proposition can be true in the world while remaining inaccessible to an internal being and unresolved by the host.

This distinction is central:

```text
truth or existence in the world
!= causal accessibility to an internal being
!= effective evaluability by the host
```

### Physical hypercomputation

A future Bubble instantiated in a physical substrate with non-standard causal resources would inherit only the capabilities justified by that substrate. A conventional simulation of a Malament-Hogarth spacetime, infinite-time machine, or exact analog device does not inherit the simulated system's idealized computational power.

## Candidate Computability Provenance

No syntax or runtime commitment is made yet. A future formal contract should at least distinguish:

- `effective`: obtained by finite effective execution;
- `relative-oracle`: obtained relative to a named oracle or cross-world computational source;
- `limit-derived`: dependent on an explicit limit-stage or fixed-point rule;
- `axiomatic`: true only relative to a declared model or axiom basis, not host-evaluated;
- `undetermined`: not resolved under the available law, evidence, and resource boundary;
- `contradicted`: inconsistent with the declared semantic contract.

Replay would differ by provenance. Effective results may be re-executed. Oracle results require preservation or independent verification of the external answer and route. Limit-derived results require their completion rule and proof dependency. Axiomatic results require their model basis. None may be silently relabeled as effective.

## Bubble-Specific Guards

- World Will may optimize only over a frontier it can lawfully evaluate. It cannot solve an undecidable global optimization problem without typed extra power.
- Positive and negative seas are not computation oracles by default.
- An anchor does not gain oracle power from its name; the relation and provenance must be explicit.
- Endogenous branching does not decide an undecidable predicate merely because all finite branches are preserved.
- Recursive self-construction can rearrange or extend available code but cannot bootstrap a non-computable source from a purely effective substrate.
- An exact value, random source, physical measurement, or learned parameter must not smuggle untracked non-computable information into the world.
- The existence of undecidable questions about a world is not itself hypercomputation by that world.

## First Research Gate

Before implementation, construct a formal comparison among:

1. one purely effective Bubble;
2. the same Bubble with a typed oracle-bearing anchor;
3. one symbolic limit-stage Bubble whose host can prove only selected finite consequences;
4. one intentionally invalid case that hides oracle information in an unexplained exact constant.

The comparison must prove which layer supplies every increase in computational power, how an internal being can causally access it, what the host can actually evaluate, what replay can reproduce, and where `undetermined` remains mandatory.

This research question is independent and unscheduled. It must not be used to skip the remaining Bubble-universe completion obligations or to claim that the present runtime is a hypercomputer.
